About Me

Hi. I am Hong Jin Jo, a Doctoral Candidate of Sociology at the University of Chicago. My research interests focus on elites, globalization, and reproduction. In particular, I examine how elites of the Third World has mobilized the globalization as a new opportunity structure for their reproduction and how their strategy has changed according to local and global social changes.
